About Me

Hi, I'm Katie Stoltz

I'm a web developer and designer passionate about creating beautiful, functional websites. With over 5 years of experience in the industry, I specialize in modern web technologies and best practices.

My goal is to help developers and designers build better websites faster and easier through tutorials, resources, and tools.

My Journey

I started my web development journey in college, where I learned the basics of HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. After graduating, I worked for several tech companies, honing my skills and learning new technologies.

In 2020, I decided to start sharing my knowledge with others through this blog. Since then, I've helped thousands of developers and designers improve their skills and create better websites.

My Approach

I believe in a user-centered approach to web development. Every website should be designed with the end user in mind, focusing on usability, accessibility, and performance.

I'm a strong advocate for clean code, responsive design, and modern web standards. I constantly stay updated with the latest trends and technologies to provide the most relevant and helpful content.
