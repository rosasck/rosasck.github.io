About Me

Hi, I'm Katie Stoltz

I'm a software developer passionate about the evolving landscape where software engineering meets AI innovation. With experience in both traditional development and emerging AI technologies, I created Stoltz Stack to explore how artificial intelligence is transforming the way we build software.

My journey in tech has given me a front-row seat to the rapid evolution from manual coding to AI-assisted development. Through this blog, I share insights, tutorials, and perspectives on AI tools, machine learning systems, and the changing practices of software engineering.

My Journey

After graduating college, I began my career at a cybersecurity startup where I quickly rose to the position of CTO. This experience gave me invaluable insights into security-minded development practices and the critical intersection of software reliability and protection against threats.

As AI technologies began transforming the development landscape, I made the decision to pivot my career toward AI development. This transition allowed me to combine my security background with cutting-edge AI tools and methodologies, giving me a unique perspective on how these technologies can work together to create more robust software solutions.

Today, I'm focused on exploring how AI is reshaping the software engineering discipline while maintaining the security principles that have guided my career from the beginning. Through Stoltz Stack, I aim to share these experiences and insights with the broader developer community.
